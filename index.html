Joint Road Forward

    • Intended Audience

      Policy makers, Transportation Industry, Academia

    • Keywords

      Mobility, infrastructure, policy.


    A project that uses gaming and computerized simulations to study planning for mobility infrastructure and policy.

    The research project is a collaboration between academia and industry in India and the Netherlands, with a goal to learn from both contexts. Leveraging different forms of relevant data, and participatory methods, we are building specific mobility models, which form the base for the gaming and simulation platform to better design mobility infrastructure and policies.
